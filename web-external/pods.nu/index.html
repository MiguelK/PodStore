    A world of podcasts

    Pods is a podcast platform that offers personal recommendations for users and supports the creators through a revenue share model, currently live in Denmark and Germany.

    As a member, you’ll have access to thousands of the best podcasts, as well as completely new, unique shows that can’t be found anywhere else. Listen when and where you want, and learn more about yourself and the world around you.

    Sign up for our newsletter and we'll write you when Pods launches in your area.

    Building an eco-system for podcasters

    Pods brings you the podcasts you already know and love, as well as lots of exclusive content from both well-known and brand new hosts.

    At Pods, our mission is to build a new, fair ecosystem for podcasters that rewards creators for making content that people want to hear. Our business model means you’re supporting your favourite podcasts every time you listen.

    We believe that all of the talented podcast creators out there deserve to be paid for their amazing content.

    Not only do we have a wide array of Pods exclusive Podcasts, but we also have all the podcasts you already know and love!
